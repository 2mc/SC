Autocousmatic Questionnaire


Thank you for trying out this program! Please do consider filling in this short questionnaire to return to me. You can save this file via the File menu and attach to an email, or just copy and paste into an email directly (My email: N dot Collins at sussex dot ac dot uk). All data will be anonymised, and there is no obligation to take part in the questionnaire, nor complete it all, though anything you can provide would be really helpful for the underlying research project. 




Q1 What is the extent of your own background in electroacoustic art music? Would you say you were an expert judge of the medium?




Q2 How much have you used the program already? Please indicate the number of separate works you have created with it (not mixes of one work, but complete runs). 




Q3 How effective do you find the final outputs of the program, as examples of art music appropriate to acousmatic presentation in an electroacoustic concert?  




Q4 Have you encountered any related projects? What are they, and how would you compare this system?

 



Q5 Do you have any further comments on this project?
