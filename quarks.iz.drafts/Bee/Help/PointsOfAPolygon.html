Accessing the polygon points of a drawing made by a Bee. 


The points of a polygon are stored in variabe lines of variable poly of a Bee. 


//:First make the drawing

b = Bee.draw({ | b, i | b.turn(pi / 6); b.move(50) }, repeats: 12, rate: 0);


//:Then after the drawing is done and all points are there, look at the poly variable of Bee

// Then print the points in the polygon in the bee: 


b.poly.lines.asCompileString;


//:You can also round the points for easier viewing if you want: 


b.poly.lines.round(0.1).asCompileString
